Welcome to Stand and Shine, a non profit organisation
formed in 2015 as a vision of God.

The organisation exists to help women in need empower themselves in
difficult situations by giving them the tools to make an impact in the world

View our Portfolio

Portfolio

Our organisation is open to people from all walks of life and is not restricted to race, creed, colour or religion. Our mission is to improve the lives of these women while providing much needed social services such as grief counselling, emotional counselling and family planning.

About Us

Our group is currently operating in Yeoville, Berea and Hilbrow in Johannesburg.
Our vision is to improve the lives of widows, the poor and single mothers with children.
- We provide social services to widows and single mothers.
- We supply basic needs in the home by providing food and clothing
- We assist with skills development.
- We provide support for abused woman and children in distress.
- We distribute food for the homeless during thefirst week of every month.
